# PowerPoint Content Guide

> [!tip] How to Use This
> Each slide corresponds to one question. Keep slides clean. Bullets only, no walls of text. You'll elaborate verbally using the script.

---

# HOLLYWOOD MARRIAGE

---

## Slide 1: What Is Hollywood Marriage?

**Title:** Hollywood Marriage

**Content:**
- Marriages between high-profile celebrities
- Defined by wealth, fame, and public scrutiny
- Often stereotyped as short-lived or superficial

**Visuals:** Collage of celebrity wedding photos (non-specific or generic silhouette)

---

## Slide 2: Historical Background

**Title:** A Brief History

**Content:**
- **1920s-1950s (Studio Era):**
  - Studios "owned" stars
  - Marriages were sometimes arranged for PR or to cover scandals

- **Modern Era:**
  - Personal choice, but heavily influenced by branding
  - Couples are viewed as business assets

**Visuals:** Old Hollywood vs. modern Hollywood split image

---

## Slide 3: The Worldview Behind It

**Title:** What Drives These Marriages?

**Content:**
- **Individualism & Careerism**
  - Career success and public image often prioritized

- **Romantic Idealism vs. Reality**
  - Media sells fairy tales
  - Reality: long-distance, temptation, no privacy

**Visuals:** Icon of a heart split with a camera/spotlight

---

## Slide 4: How to Talk to These Couples

**Title:** Communication Tips

**Register to Use:**
- Professional but casual
- Treat them as peers, not celebrities
- Focus on work, hobbies (not fame)

**Taboo Topics:**
- "Is this a publicity stunt?"
- "Did you sign a prenup?"
- Comparisons to exes
- Tabloid gossip

**Visuals:** Speech bubble icons or conversation imagery

---

## Slide 5: Real-Life Examples

**Title:** Famous Hollywood Marriages

| Couple                            | Notable For                                     |
| --------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------- |
| Elizabeth Taylor & Richard Burton | Married/divorced twice. Defined "power couple." |
| Kim Kardashian & Kris Humphries   | 72-day marriage. Reality TV spectacle.          |
| Britney Spears & Jason Alexander  | 55-hour Vegas marriage. Annulled immediately.   |

**Visuals:** 3-column layout with images (if available) or names only

---

## Slide 6: Dynamics of the Marriage

**Title:** What's It Really Like?

**Content:**
- **Public Ownership:** Everyone feels entitled to judge
- **Lack of Privacy:** Paparazzi capture everything
- **Competitive Egos:** Both chasing success, sometimes at odds

**Visuals:** Paparazzi camera icon, spotlight, or "vs." graphic

# SAME-SEX MARRIAGE

---

## Slide 7: What Is Same-Sex Marriage?

**Title:** Same-Sex Marriage

**Content:**
- Legal union between two people of the same gender
- Equal to opposite-sex marriage under the law

**Visuals:** Rainbow flag or equality symbol (subtle, professional)

---

## Slide 8: Historical Background

**Title:** The Road to Recognition

**Content:**
- Historically criminalized or labeled a mental illness
- **Stonewall Riots (1969):** Catalyst for modern LGBTQ+ rights movement
- **2001:** Netherlands legalizes same-sex marriage (first country)
- **2015:** *Obergefell v. Hodges*, US Supreme Court ruling

**Visuals:** Timeline or milestone markers

---

## Slide 9: The Worldview Behind It

**Title:** What Drives These Marriages?

**Content:**
- **Egalitarianism:**
  - Equal rights (taxes, inheritance, hospital visitation)

- **Secularism/Humanism:**
  - Civil marriage as a human right, separate from religion

**Visuals:** Scales of justice or equality icons

---

## Slide 10: How to Talk to These Couples

**Title:** Communication Tips

**Register to Use:**
- Inclusive and open
- Use neutral terms ("partner," "spouse") until corrected
- Ask about their journey, not the mechanics

**Taboo Topics:**
- "Who's the man/woman?"
- Invasive questions about intimacy or biology
- "When did you decide to be gay?"

**Visuals:** Respectful dialogue imagery (two speech bubbles)

---

## Slide 11: Real-Life Examples

**Title:** Notable Same-Sex Marriages

| Couple | Notable For |
|--------|-------------|
| Ellen DeGeneres & Portia de Rossi | Helped normalize LGBTQ+ relationships in mainstream media |
| Elton John & David Furnish | Among the first UK civil partnerships; major advocates |
| Neil Patrick Harris & David Burtka | Publicly raising twins; normalized gay parenting |

**Visuals:** 3-column layout with names or images

---

## Slide 12: Dynamics of the Marriage

**Title:** What's It Really Like?

**Content:**
- **Negotiated Roles:** Chores and responsibilities split by ability, not gender
- **Resilience:** Bond formed through navigating discrimination together
- **Chosen Family:** Close friends as extended family

**Visuals:** Hands holding, family icon, or support network graphic

---

## Slide 13: Closing / Thank You

**Title:** Thank You!

**Content:**
- Questions?
- (Optional: Group members' names)

**Visuals:** Simple, clean. Maybe a subtle graphic of both topics.

---

> [!important] Design Tips
> - Use **consistent fonts** and color schemes across all slides.
> - Keep bullet points SHORT. 5-7 words max per line.
> - Avoid clip art. Use clean icons or real images.
> - **Less is more.** Let the speaker (you) do the explaining.
