### Final Grade Calculation

- **Final Exam (F)** = 25% of the final grade
- **Average of Prelim, Midterm, and Pre-Final (A)** = 75% of the final grade, where
$$
A \;=\; \frac{\text{Prelim} + \text{Midterm} + \text{Pre-Final}}{3}
$$
The final grade (G) is computed as:
$$
G \;=\; 0.25\,F \; + \; 0.75\,A
$$
Or, substituting the average explicitly:
$$
G \;=\; 0.25F \; + \; 0.75\left(\frac{P + M + PF}{3}\right)
$$
