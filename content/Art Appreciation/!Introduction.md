> > Upon successful completion of the course, students will be able to:

> > - Exploring the Creative Process

> [!note]+ Module & Assessment Structure
> **The Learning Framework (Pre-lectio, Lectio, Repetitio)**
> Each module follows this cycle:
> 1. **Pre-lectio:** Materials you are expected to go through *prior* to the F2F meeting.
> 2. **Lectio:** Student-teacher interaction in the classroom (discussion strategies) to deepen understanding of pre-lectio materials.
> 3. **Repetitio:** Learning assessment activities (graded or non-graded).
>

> **Grading System & Computations**
> The course is divided into three grading periods: **Prelim**, **Midterm**, and **Prefinal**.
>
> > [!chart] Grading Formula
> > **PAA** = Preparatory Assessment Activities (Module Assessments)
> > **FSA** = Final Summative Assessment
> >
> > | Period Grade | Weighting |
> > | :--- | :--- |
> > | **Prelim** | **40%** PAA + **60%** FSA |
> > | **Midterm** | **40%** PAA + **60%** FSA |
> > | **Prefinal** | **40%** PAA + **60%** FSA |
> >
> > **Final Course Grade:**
> > $\text{Final Grade} = \frac{\text{Prelim} + \text{Midterm} + \text{Prefinal}}{3}$
> 
> > [!sparkle] Final Project: The Art Talk
> > The final summative assessment is an **Art Talk**. 
> >
> > This is an articulation of your specific artistic expression. Whether you are into **singing, dancing, poetry, graphic design, video editing, directing, acting, color grading, photography**, etc., this is your chance to showcase your **creative process** and demonstrate it in class.
